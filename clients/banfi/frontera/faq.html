Frequently Asked Questions

How do I qualify for the $25 live event ticket credit?

In order to qualify, you must visit a participating retailer within the United States to receive a bottle neck hanger from a specially marked bottle of Frontera wine during the promotional period, November 1, 2015 and March 31, 2016. To receive the reward, you must complete and submit an online Registration Form on the Register page no later than 11:59PM ET on March 31, 2016. In order to complete the online Registration Form, you will need the Registration Code located on a bottle neck hanger, the UPC code (located on your Frontera wine bottle), and willneed to confirm that you have read and accepted all promotional Terms and Conditions.

How many rewards can I receive?

There is a limit of one reward per customer, per customer household, per booking group.

Where can I find my Registration Code in order to submit the online Registration Form?

Your Registration Code can be found on the back side of your Frontera $25 live event ticket credit bottle neck hanger. Your code will expire after 11:59PM ET on March 31, 2016.

Is wine purchase necessary to obtain a Frontera UPC code to redeem the $25 live event ticket credit from Ticket Network offer?

No, by law, wine purchase is not necessary to redeem promotional offers.

What is TicketNetwork?

TicketNetwork.com is an online marketplace that connects buyers and sellers of live event tickets allowing you to save on sports, concert, theater events, and much more. They provide fans (like you!) with access to a huge selection of quality event tickets, even when they are sold out elsewhere.

How does TicketNetwork work?

You will need to first create an account on TicketNetwork.com/Frontera. You will then select your event of choice and your seat then proceed to check out where you will have the opportunity to apply your $25 ticket credit code. If you have more questions, please visit, http://www.ticketnetwork.com/help.aspx.

Are there any restrictions to the $25 live event ticket credit?

You must be 21 years of age or older in order to redeem for this reward. This reward is valid November 1, 2015 through March 31, 2016 at www.ticketnetwork.com/frontera. In order to qualify, you must first visit a participating retail stores within the United States to receive a bottle neck hanger from a specially marked bottle of Frontera wine. There is a limit of one reward per customer, per customer household, per booking group.

To qualify for the $25 live ticket credit from TicketNetwork offer, visit the Register page to submit the online Registration Form no later than 11:59PM ET March 31, 2016. You must redeem your credit code at TicketNetwork.com/Frontera no later than 11:59PM PT on May 31, 2015. Rewards are subject to availability. Please review the Terms and Conditions for complete details.

How long do I have to use my $25 live event ticket credit code?

Once you complete and submit your online Registration Form, you will automatically receive an email from mywinerewards@rewardrequest.com with a link to TicketNetwork.com/Frontera and a $25.00 USD live event ticket credit code. You must redeem your credit code at TicketNetwork.com/Frontera no later than 11:59PM PT on May 31, 2015.
