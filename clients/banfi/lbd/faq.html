Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I qualify for a $25 credit towards a round of golf from Golfweek?

    In order to qualify, you must visit a participating retailer within the United States to receive a bottle neck hanger from a specially marked bottle of Gran Reserva wine during the promotional period, May 1, 2016 and July 31, 2016. To receive the reward, you must complete and submit an online Registration Form on the Register page no later than 11:59PM PT on July 31, 2016. In order to complete the online Registration Form, you will need the Registration Code located on a bottle neck hanger, the UPC code (located on your Gran Reserva wine bottle), and will need to confirm that you have read and accepted all promotional terms and conditions.



  • How many rewards can I receive?

    There is a limit of one (1) reward per Customer name, per Customer email address, per Customer booking group. Even if you obtain multiple neck hangers, you will still only receive one reward.



  • Where can I find my Registration Code in order to submit the online Registration Form?

    Your Registration Code can be found on the back side of your Gran Reserva bottle neck hanger. You must submit your Registration Code on the Register page no later than 11:59PM PT July 31, 2016 in order to redeem your reward.



  • Is wine purchase necessary to obtain a Gran Reserva UPC code?

    No, by law, wine purchase is not necessary to redeem promotional offers.



  • Are there any restrictions to this Reward?

    You must be 21 years of age or older in order to redeem for this reward. This reward is valid between May 1, 2015 and July 31, 2016. In order to qualify, you must first visit a participating retail stores within the United States to receive a bottle neck hanger from a specially marked bottle of Gran Reserva wine. There is a limit of one reward per Customer name, per Customer email address, per Customer booking group.

    To qualify for the $25 credit from Golfweek offer, visit the Register page to submit the online Registration Form no later than 11:59PM PT on July 31, 2016. You must redeem your credit code at www.golfweek.com/teetimes no later than 11:59PM PT on August 31, 2016. Rewards are subject to availability. Please review the Terms and Conditions for complete details.



  • How long do I have to use my $25 credit code?

    Once you complete and submit your online Registration Form, you will automatically receive an email from mywinerewards@rewardrequest.com with a link to Golfweek and a $25.00 USD credit code. You must redeem your credit code at Golfweek no later than 11:59PM PT on August 31, 2016.



  • If I have another question that is not answered in these FAQs, who can I contact to help me with my question?

    If your question has not been answered in the provided FAQs, please email us at mywinerewards@rewardrequest.com or call us at 617-939-0372 9AM – 6PM ET, excluding public holidays. We will be happy to answer any other questions you may have.
