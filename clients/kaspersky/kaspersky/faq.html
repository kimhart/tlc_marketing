Frequently Asked Questions

GENERAL FAQs

How do I qualify for the rewards?

In order to qualify, you must purchase a select, qualifying renewal and/or upgrade of Kaspersky Lab software between May, 23 2016 and October 15, 2015. To receive the reward, you must complete and submit the Registration Form on the Redeem page no later than 11:59 P.M. PT on October 30, 2016. In order to complete the Registration Form, you will need the Unique Code that is found in the qualifying email from Kaspersky Lab, and you will need to confirm that you have read and accepted all promotional Terms and Conditions. After the form is filled out in its entirety, you will then be able to choose a reward. If you choose a voucher reward (Health & Wellness or Fun Pass), you will receive an email with an attached voucher. Print the voucher and bring it to your scheduled appointment along with a valid photo ID. If you choose a digital reward (Rhapsody Premier Music Subscription or Smilebooks Canvas), you will automatically receive a Reward Code and directions on how to redeem on the partner site.

How many Rewards am I entitled to?

You are entitled to one reward per customer, per customer household, per booking group.

How old do I have to be in order to redeem the reward?

You must be eighteen (18) years or older to redeem the reward.

There is no venue available in my area for the Health & Wellness and Fun Pass Rewards. What do I do?

If there is no venue available in your area for the Health & Wellness and Fun Pass rewards, you are entitled to choose from one of our digital rewards.

I changed my mind and want to exchange my Reward for another. What do I do?

Sorry, your Reward selection is final.

Are there any restrictions to this promotion?

You must be eighteen (18) years of age or older to redeem any Reward. The value and types of Rewards will vary depending on the geographic location of customers as well as the specific vendor fulfilling the Reward. Voucher Rewards (Fun Pass and Health & Wellness) and Rhapsody valid in the continental United States (excluding U.S. territories) only. Smilebooks and Digital Fitness Classes are valid in the Continental United States (excluding U.S. territories) and Canada. Rewards are not transferable and customer’s Reward selection is final. Vouchers are valid for three months from the date of issue. The voucher expiration date is displayed on the voucher. All Unique Codes must be entered onto the microsite by 11:59PM PT on October 15, 2016. Smilebooks and Rhapsody Reward codes will expire after 11:59PM November 15, 2016. All Rewards are available until supplies last and are further subject to promotional availability. Please refer to the Terms & Conditions page for full promotional terms as well as specific Reward terms.

If I have another question that is not answered in these FAQs, who can I contact to help me with my question?

If your question has not been answered in the provided FAQs, please call us at 617-939-0391 Monday – Friday 9AM – 6PM ET (excluding holidays) or email us at KasperskySecureTheFun@rewardrequest.com. We will be happy to answer any other questions you may have.
