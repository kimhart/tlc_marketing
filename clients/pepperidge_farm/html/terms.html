General Eligibility and Redemption Terms & Conditions

  1. This Reward requires a purchase of three (3) loaves of Pepperidge Farm® Whole Grain or Farmhouse bread at participating retail locations between 4/19/16 and 6/19/16 ("Final Purchase Date") (“Qualifying Purchase”).
  2. To receive the reward, the Consumer must make a Qualified Purchase and visit the promotional microsite to create an account with their name, email address, DOB, phone number, and password. Consumer must upload a photo of their receipt(s) showing the Qualifying Purchase on the Get Started page. If the Qualifying Purchase isn’t made in the same transaction, Consumer must upload the individual receipts at different times so long as the final receipt is uploaded and submitted no later than 11:59PM PT on 7/1/16 (Registration). Within 5 business days of receiving the Registration, Promoter (namely The Reward Company, Inc, d/b/a TLC Marketing) will email each qualified Consumer a link to the Microsite with a unique code. The unique code is required to complete redemption and expires at 11:59PM PT on 7/31/16. Consumer must return to the microsite and log in, enter their ZIP code and unique code in order to search for venues listed in their geographical area. Consumer is then emailed a voucher to their preferred venue.
  3. Picture of receipt(s) that are uploaded must be clear and legible. The time and date of purchase as well as the qualifying products must be circled. If Consumer submits receipt(s) that is not legible they will have one more chance to re-submit before becoming disqualified and ineligible to the Reward.
  4. The Reward is only valid for one (1) Reward redemption per person, per email address and per booking group (group is defined as more than one person accompanying the voucher holder). Multiple redemptions shall not be redeemed and Promoter has no obligation whatsoever to honor any offers accepted in violation of these Terms and Conditions. All repeat visits will cost full price. Anyone accompanying the voucher holder must pay the regular price for their service.
  5. All Rewards are available until supplies last and are further subject to promotional availability. Should a requested Reward not be available at time of redemption by Consumer, then The Reward Company, Inc, d/b/a TLC Marketing Worldwide ("Promoter") may offer the Consumer an alternative Reward of comparable value. Each Reward is subject to further definition and may have additional terms and conditions governing the redemption thereof. Consumer must be eighteen (18) years of age or older to redeem any Reward. Void where prohibited or restricted by law. The value and types of Rewards will vary depending on the geographic location of Consumer as well as the specific vendor fulfilling the Reward. Rewards are not transferable and Consumer’s Reward selection is final. Vouchers are valid for use for three (3) months from date of issue. The promoter reserves the right to change or modify these General Terms as well as the terms and conditions of the specific Reward at any time during the Promotion. It shall be the Consumer’s sole obligation to check these General Terms and any applicable terms and conditions during the Promotion and upon Reward redemption.
