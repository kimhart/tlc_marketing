Upload Your Receipts

Complete your registration by uploading and submitting your
receipt(s) by 11:59PM PT on 7/1/16. Don’t forget to circle the qualifying
items, date, and time of purchase on your receipt.

Please also make sure the picture of your receipt is legible and shows the required information (date, time, and qualifying products). If we cannot read your receipt, it will not count towards the 3 loaf requirement. You are allowed one resubmission per receipt that is deemed illegible. You must resubmit all receipts by 11:59PM PT 7/1/16.

Receive Your Unique Code

Upon receiving your receipt(s), we will send you an email with a unique code and link to the Redeem page. Allow approximately 5 business days.

Redeem Your Reward

Once on the Redeem page, enter your ZIP code and unique code to select your FREE* reward. Remember, your unique code will expire at 11:59PM PT 7/31/16.
