Get Started




1

Purchase a pair of Carrera Sunglasses or Optical Frames

from a participating eye care professional or retail location between June 15, 2016 and August 15, 2016 and receive a 'Thank You' Voucher with a Unique Code.

Please make sure you read the full Terms & Conditions.

2

Complete application & upload receipt

Using the Unique Code from your 'Thank You' Voucher, please complete the online Application Form below and upload the original store receipt showing your qualifying purchase.

In order to complete the online Application Form, you will need the model number of your sunglasses or optical frames and will need to confirm that you have read and accepted all promotional Terms & Conditions. Please note, all applications must be uploaded within fourteen (14) days of purchase as indicated on your receipt.

3

Get your reward code

Within five (5) business days of receiving and validating your application, we will email you a Reward Code to submit on the Redeem page.

Your Reward Code will expire at 11:59PM PT on 9/14/16.

4

Choose theater location

Choose a theater chain in your area and click submit! Within five (5) business days of redeeming, you will be mailed your free movie ticket.

5

Enjoy!

Bring your movie ticket to the selected theater and enjoy your movie of choice!
