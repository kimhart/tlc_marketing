Frequently Asked Questions

How do I qualify for the one (1) free movie ticket from Carrera Reward?

In order to qualify, you must purchase a pair of Carrera Sunglasses or Optical Frames between June 15, 2016 and August 15, 2016. At the time your qualifying purchase is made, you will be given a Thank You Voucher which includes a Unique Code. Using your Unique Code, you will submit an online Application Form which can be found on the How To Get Started page. You will be instructed to fill out your contact information and upload the original store receipt showing your qualifying purchase within fourteen (14) days of purchase (as indicated on your receipt). Within five (5) business days of receiving and validating your proof of purchase, we will email you a Reward Code and a link back to the Redeem page where you will be instructed to enter the Reward Code, your contact information, postal code and choose your preferred theater chain no later than 11:59PM PT on August 30, 2016. Within five (5) business days of selecting and confirming your theater chain of choice, we will mail out your movie ticket.

What do I need in order to apply for the reward?

You will need the Thank You Voucher that you received in store, your original receipt showing proof of purchase and the model number of your sunglasses or optical frame.

How long do I have to submit my proof of purchase?

You must complete the Application Form in its entirety and upload your original receipt within fourteen (14) days of purchase. Please have your Unique Code available from the Thank You Voucher received in store as you will need it to complete the Application Form.

How long do I have to submit my Reward Code?

Once your proof of purchase and Application Form is validated, we will send you an email with your Reward Code. You must enter your Reward Code and your contact information on the Redeem page by 11:59PM PT on 9/14/16 in order to receive your free movie ticket.

When can I expect to receive my movie ticket?

Within five (5) business days of submitting yourReward Code and contact information on the Redeem page, you will be mailed your movie ticket. Please note, your movie ticket will be mailed via standard postage.

Are there any restrictions to this offer?

Offer valid with the purchase of one (1) complete pair of Carrera Sunglasses or Optical Frames between June 15, 2016 and August 15, 2016. Limit of one (1) Reward per customer and per customer household. Movie restrictions apply. Reward is valid while supplies last and is subject to promotional availability. Applications required within fourteen (14) days of purchase. Unique Codes are not valid after 11:59PM PT on 8/30/16. Reward codes are not valid after 11:59PM PT on 9/14/16. The types of movies and movie theaters will vary dependent on the geographic location. Reward is non-transferable and has no cash value. Void if transferred, sold, auctioned, photocopied or otherwise altered and where prohibited or restricted by law. Customer must be eighteen (18) years or older in order to redeem. Offer valid in the United States only (excluding U.S. territories) (Canada) only. Please refer to the Terms and Conditions page for further restrictions.

What does this movie ticket entitle me to?

The movie ticket entitles you to one (1) movie ticket to be used at a participating theater. Additional tickets, as well as food, beverages, taxes, fees and other entertainment available at the theater are not included.

Can I sell my movie ticket?

No, your movie ticket cannot be sold for cash.

I changed my mind and want to go to a different movie theater, what do I do?

Sorry, your initial movie theater choice is final. Your movie ticket is nonrefundable.

Does the movie ticket voucher have an expiration date?

In the United States the movie ticket does not have an expiration date. In Canada, the movie ticket will expire after two years, excluding Quebec and Saskatchewan.

What are the participating Cinemas included in this Reward?

The participating cinemas in the United States are AMC, Cinemark and Carmike. The participating Cinema in Canada is Cineplex.

How old do I have to be to redeem a movie ticket Reward?

You must be eighteen (18) years or older in order to redeem a movie ticket reward.

How many times can I qualify to receive the one free movie ticket?

You can only qualify once. The reward is valid for one (1) reward per customer, per household.
